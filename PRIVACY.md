# Privacy Policy — Crown Breaker

_Last updated: June 2026_

Crown Breaker is designed with your privacy in mind. **The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Crown Breaker does not collect any personal information or usage data.

## Data stored on your device

- Your **high score** and level progress are saved **locally on your Apple Watch only**. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## What we don't do

- No accounts or sign-in
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No third-party SDKs
- No network requests — the game runs entirely offline on your watch

## Children's privacy

Because Crown Breaker collects no data of any kind, it collects no data from children.
